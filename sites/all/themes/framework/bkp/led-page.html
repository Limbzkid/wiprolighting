How to Choose Good LED Luminaire

LED has become the new synonym for a sustainable & green lighting solution and away of life across indoor and outdoor spaces. With growing demand in the market and multiple manufacturers offering a variety of LED solutions, the market is moving at a rapid pace. In such a scenario, it becomes important to know the key promoters in technology and have an evaluation system which would help the users in choosing a right LED lighting solution.

  • The LED manufacturer and the patents held.
  • The smaller the binning tolerance, the better the performance.
  • The higher lumen output for the right light and significant energy saving.
  • The right colour temperature for quality, consistency and reliability.
  • LM80 compliance ensures a functional LED life.
